What is a starter Pokémon?

A starter Pokémon is the first Pokémon that a Pokémon Trainer obtains at the start of their Pokémon journey, most commonly gifted to them by a Pokémon Professor, parent, or teacher, or encountered in the wild near the town in which the trainer begins their journey. This Pokémon will be used to battle the first wild Pokémon that a Trainer encounters.
